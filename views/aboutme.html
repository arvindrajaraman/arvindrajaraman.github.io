Advisors: Kurt Keutzer, Amir Gholami

Problem Statement: Text generation models on the market today (LSTMs, Transformers, GPT-2, etc.) either have low latency or high accuracy, but usually not both. Because of the wide range of edge devices on the market today, some models would benefit from an architecture "in the middle", with both latency and accuracy constraints. How do we design models to fit this spectrum of constraints?

  • Designing a hybrid architecture using LSTM encoding and attention-based decoding, significantly decreasing latency.
  • Implementing new model compression techniques: neural architecture search (NAS), knowledge distillation, pruning, and quantization.
  • Determining the Pareto frontier of accuracy versus latency on different architecture designs.
